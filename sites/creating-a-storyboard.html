Time Capsule Templates

A Video Time Capsule can be created at life's major events, every ten years for yourself, for your child, or done just once, as the record of You.

It's up to you - all you have to do is choose the template that is right for you

Choose from our templates


Generate your script

Generate your personalized storyboard

Once you have chosen your template, you should decide on the key messages you want to cover in each interview segment. You can type in and save the "prompts" for these when you are logged into your account online.

Once you are ready to film, login to the app and the script will be there ready for you to use on the teleprompter.
